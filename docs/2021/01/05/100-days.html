100 Days of Audiovisual Composition

Jan 5, 2021

itp audiovis 100_days

After exploring some basic audio visualization systems in 2020 (Rings and Saber), I dived into a daily audiovisual composition challenge starting in 2021.

Click here to view all on Instagram.

Rules

  1. On A Day, compose an original audio composition of no more than 30 seconds, using only one instrument or synth patch. No presets!
  2. On B Day, create a video composition accompaniment.
  3. Repeat 50 times, beginning on January 1st.
