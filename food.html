  • Pizza

    Osteria Procaccini in Kingston NJ, the most delicious pizza ever. Close second is Nomad, in Hopewell. Honorable mentions include Modern a Pizza in New Haven

  • Burgers

    It's quite interesting how a new kind of burger joint has sprung up in the past two years. Five Guys, Shake Shack, In-n-out... They're all solid restaurants. But the best burger place I have ever been to is Umami Burger in Los Angeles. It's a little on the pricey side and a lot pretentious (think tiny, artfully arranged plates with a lot of negative space), but oh my so delicious. The restuarant grinds their own meat from choice cuts of steak and the shiitake mushrooms are yummy to say the least.

  • Ice Cream

    For a long time the Bent Spoon in Princeton NJ took the crown for hands-down, richest, most delicious, locally sourced and bursting with flavor ice cream place. But on a recent visit to Montreal, I think Kem Coba could really give them a run for their money. The intensity of flavor is really impossible to describe; you'll have to go and see for yourself.

  • Donuts

    The Donut Man, in Pasadena CA wins this one. Screw Dunkin Donuts.
