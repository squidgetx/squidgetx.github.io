  • 05-29 - Review: The Pantry (breakfast) 7/10

    I love eggs, and I love lox. So it goes that I would probably love lox with eggs, a la the popular diner dish often named as the lox scrambler. Unfortunately, this is not the case and I really need to stop ordering this dish. Still, The Pantry's rendition was fairly yummy and as many users on Yelp note, their home fries are delicious. Browned and not too oily, with (maybe a little bit too much) salt, yum. Up past Modern a Pizza, this place is pretty far so not that easy to get to. Price wise it is fair, especially if you go for the pancakes or waffles. They tricked me into ordering toast on the side though, which ended up costing extra.

    Lox Scrambler (with home fries), rye toast, and a nice glass of OJ: $15 incl. tip

    Food: 7/10

    Service: 6/10

    Price: 7/10

    Atmosphere: 8/10
